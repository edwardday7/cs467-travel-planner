About

The Crowd-Sourced Travel Planner is an app that allows users to plan their trips around amazing experiences wherever they go. Users can create their own experiences by adding a title, description, geo-location, image, and rating. Users can also discover new experiences by searching by location or keyword, and viewing them in a list. Users can click on any experience to see more details and rate it. Users can also create their own trips by adding a name and adding experiences to them. Users can view, edit, or delete their trips at any time.
